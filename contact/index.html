Response Time

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24-48 hours during weekdays. Response times may be slightly longer on weekends. For urgent matters, please include "URGENT" in your subject line.

When contacting us about technical issues, please include:

  • The device you're using (PC, Chromebook, mobile, etc.)
  • Browser and version (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, etc.)
  • Detailed description of the issue
  • Screenshots if possible (attach to email)

Game Suggestions

We're always looking for new games to add to our collection! If you have a game you'd like to see on 1v1 LOL Unblocked, please let us know. Our team reviews all suggestions and prioritizes based on:

Popular Demand

Games requested by multiple users get higher priority in our review process.

Technical Feasibility

We evaluate whether the game can run smoothly on school networks and Chromebooks.

Appropriateness

All games must be school-appropriate and comply with our content guidelines.

While we can't promise to add every suggested game, we do read and consider all submissions. Popular requests often get added within 2-3 weeks!
