How to Play Tag

Controls: Use arrow keys or WASD to move your character. The player who is "it" will be highlighted - chase others to tag them. When you're "it," avoid being tagged for as long as possible to score points.

Objective: Score points by surviving as the non-tagged player for extended periods or by tagging multiple players quickly when you're "it." The player with the highest score wins!

Pro Tip: Use the environment to your advantage - corners and obstacles can help you evade pursuers or trap opponents when you're "it."
